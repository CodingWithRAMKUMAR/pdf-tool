About PDFixit

PDFixit was created by Ram – a passionate developer who believes that essential PDF tools should be free, private, and accessible to everyone.

All tools run entirely in your browser. No files are ever uploaded to any server. Your documents stay on your device – always.

Why PDFixit?

  • ✅ 100% free – no hidden costs, no premium tiers.
  • 🔒 100% private – no uploads, no tracking.
  • ⚡ Blazing fast – client‑side processing.
  • 🎨 Beautiful, futuristic design.

I built PDFixit to help students, professionals, and anyone who needs to work with PDFs without worrying about privacy or subscriptions.

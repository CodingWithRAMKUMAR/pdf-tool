Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. No Personal Data Collection

PDFixit does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All PDF processing happens entirely in your browser – no files are uploaded to any server.

2. Local Processing Only

All PDF operations (merge, split, compress, etc.) are performed locally using JavaScript libraries. Your documents never leave your device.

3. No Cookies

We do not use cookies for tracking. However, third‑party services like Google Fonts may set their own cookies – we have no control over those.

4. Third‑Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from minors.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page.
